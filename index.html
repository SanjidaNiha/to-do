Dynamic To-Do List

    User Guide

    Adding a New Task: Type your task in the input box and click the "Add" button.

    Showing Your To-Do List: Click the "Show My To-Do List" button to view or hide your saved tasks.

    Marking a Task as Done: Click the check sign (✓) next to a task to mark it as done and remove it from your list.

    Button Functionality Recap:

    • Add Button: Adds a new task.
    • Show My To-Do List Button: Shows or hides your tasks.
    • Check Sign (✓) Button: Deletes a task once completed.
